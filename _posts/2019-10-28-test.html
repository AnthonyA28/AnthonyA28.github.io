  1. The requirements for Constant molar overflow are

    • The column must be adiabatic. The column is insulated well enough for this assumption to be valid.

    • The two components must have similar heats of vaporization.

      The of Isopropanol is 39.85 (KJ/mol) and of 2-Butanol is 43.3 (KJ/mol) at 255 K [1]. These values are close enough for this assumption to be valid.

    • Enthalpies are mostly affected by phase change as opposed to temperature change.

      The of Isopropanol is 83.72 (J/mol-K) and the of 2-butanol is 105.90 (J/mol-K) at 273 K. The values are approximately 1000 times smaller than the values.
